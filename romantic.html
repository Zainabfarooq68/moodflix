Let These Movies Break You Gently 🎬

The Notebook

*The Notebook* is a romantic drama about a young couple whose love story spans decades, tested by time, class differences, and memory loss.

Pride And Prejudice

*Pride and Prejudice* is a classic romance where spirited Elizabeth Bennet navigates love, class, and misunderstandings with the proud but noble Mr. Darcy.

Notting Hill

*Notting Hill* is a charming romantic comedy about a humble bookstore owner whose life changes when he falls in love with a famous movie star.

Past Lives

*Past Lives* is a poignant drama about childhood friends reunited years later, exploring love, fate, and the lives they might have lived.

Silver Linings Playbook

*Silver Linings Playbook* is a heartfelt dramedy about two troubled souls who find healing, hope, and unexpected love through dance and second chances.

Me Before You

*Me Before You* is a bittersweet romance about a spirited young woman who becomes a caregiver for a paralyzed man, leading to a life-changing bond.

The Holiday

*The Holiday* is a feel-good romantic comedy where two women swap homes across the globe and unexpectedly find love during the Christmas season.

The Age of adaline

*The Age of Adaline* is a romantic fantasy about a woman who mysteriously stops aging and must choose between eternal solitude and the risk of love.

The Longest Ride

*The Longest Ride* is a romantic drama that weaves together the lives of a bull rider and a college student, exploring love, sacrifice, and the power of memories.

Titanic

*Titanic* is an epic romance and disaster film about a young couple from different social classes who fall in love aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic.

The Vow

*The Vow* is a touching romance about a husband’s relentless effort to help his wife regain her memory and their love after a tragic accident.

Love Aaj Kal

Love Aaj Kal is a romantic drama that contrasts two love stories from different eras, showing how the challenges of love remain timeless despite changing times.

To All The Boys I've Loved Before

*To All the Boys I’ve Loved Before* is a sweet teen romantic comedy about a girl's secret love letters that accidentally get sent to her crushes, turning her life upside down.

Love,Rosie

*Love, Rosie* is a heartfelt romantic comedy about lifelong friends navigating missed opportunities, misunderstandings, and the possibility of true love.

Before Sunrise

*Before Sunrise* is a romantic drama about two strangers who meet on a train and spend a magical night walking and talking through Vienna, forming a deep connection.

Barfi

*Barfi!* is a heartwarming romantic comedy-drama about a deaf-mute man whose joyful spirit and unconventional love stories touch the lives of those around him.
